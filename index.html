Labrador Colors

Black Labrador

The most common and traditional color. Black Labs are elegant, shiny, and have a classic appearance that never goes out of style.

Yellow Labrador

Ranging from pale cream to golden fox red, yellow Labs are cheerful and bright. Their sunny disposition matches their beautiful coat.

Chocolate Labrador

The rarest of the three colors, chocolate Labs have rich, warm brown coats. They're just as loving and energetic as their black and yellow siblings.

Labrador Care Guide

Essential Care Tips

  • Provide 60-90 minutes of daily exercise to keep your Lab healthy and happy
  • Feed high-quality dog food appropriate for their age and activity level
  • Regular grooming helps manage shedding and keeps their coat healthy
  • Early socialization and training are crucial for a well-behaved adult dog
  • Regular veterinary check-ups and vaccinations are essential
  • Provide plenty of mental stimulation through toys and interactive games
  • Monitor their weight as Labs are prone to obesity
  • Dental care is important - brush their teeth regularly
